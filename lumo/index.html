[ Lumo ! ]

Scientific Name: Luminescent Rosaflexa

or just lumo hehe

Discovered by / at:

It was first discovered in 2026 as a photonic lifeform that produces light and reflects pink color. Lumo was first discovered during an interactive experiment involving light.

Family:

Strong emotional events may cause photonic fragmentation and these fragments of energy could form into new Luminescent Rosaflexa species

Habitat:

Areas saturated with prismatic light. It lives in desaturated and silent world, where all the color and light lost it emotion

Appearance:

Its body is composed of photonic energy, which appears slightly pink and almost colorless in neutral light. Its color changes based on a concentrated light source. It shares strange resemblance to Jigglypuff

Key Characteristics:

  • Lumo cannot generate its own color.
  • Direct contact with light source makes it overstimulated.
  • He is cute

What we know so far about Lumo? It is really cute and friendly! It does not like being touched, because it doesn’t have a protective layer - it is a pure light. It is a very emotional creature, it just loves light and color, even if he doesn’t produce ones. It shares it beautiful glow, gleaming in a silent and melancholic environment it lives in
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature It can turn to your mouse, which is a concentrated light source! It increases saturation with the distance between the light and its body. Don’t get too close, or else Lumo will become overwhelmed and spiky!!
